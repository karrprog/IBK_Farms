About Us

History

IBK farms is a new operation founded by Nathaniel and Heather Karr when it was purchased from Nathaniel's grandmother in October 2019. It is a a 40 acre farm that has been in the family for more than 80 years. Nathaniel and Heather are still in the process of figuring out where they fit in the farming community, but have found an interest in Rabbits and especially bees. While they are just getting started they have found that being on this farm is the most rewarding and exciting thing they have done and are grateful for the opportunity turn the family farm into something more. They are excited about this adventure and look forward to sharing it with you.
